An environmental disaster

One of the worst man-made catastrophes of the 20th century, which is still ongoing

Vanished. Salinized. Crisis.

Once vast and thriving, the Aral Sea has nearly vanished.

Soviet irrigation projects diverted rivers, causing ecological and economic collapse.

Fishing towns were abandoned as salinity levels destroyed marine life.

Restoration efforts began, but only parts of the sea are recovering.

Visiting the Aral Sea and seeing what was the fourth largest inland sea in the world almost dead has caused a tremendous shock. This is probably the biggest ecological catastrophe of our time. And it demonstrates that men can destroy the planet.
